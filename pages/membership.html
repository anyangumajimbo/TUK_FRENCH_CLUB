Frequently Asked Questions

Do I need to know French to join the club?
Not at all! We welcome students of all proficiency levels, from complete beginners to advanced speakers. Our activities cater to various levels, and we focus on creating a supportive environment for everyone to learn and improve.
How often does the club meet?
We have regular weekly meetings on Wednesdays at 2:00 PM. Additionally, we organize various activities throughout the week, such as conversation circles, film screenings, and cultural workshops. Check our activities calendar for details.
What is the difference between regular and premium membership?
Both memberships offer the same access to club activities and benefits. The premium membership includes an official TUK French Club t-shirt and priority registration for limited-capacity events.
How can I pay the membership fee?
You can pay via M-Pesa to our club treasurer or in person during our weekly meetings. After submitting your registration form, you'll receive payment instructions via email.
Can I join the club mid-semester?
Yes! We accept new members throughout the academic year. The membership fee remains the same regardless of when you join, and your membership will be valid until the end of the academic year.
Are there additional costs for club activities?
Most club activities on campus are included in your membership. Certain special events, such as cultural excursions or camping trips, may have additional costs, but these are always optional and communicated well in advance.

What We Do

Discover our diverse range of activities designed to enhance your French language skills and provide immersive cultural experiences.

French Conversation

Weekly conversation circles for members to practice speaking French in a relaxed environment, catering to all proficiency levels.

Film Screenings

Regular screenings of French films followed by discussions to help members understand French culture and improve listening skills.

Culinary Workshops

Hands-on cooking sessions where members learn to prepare authentic French dishes while practicing their language skills.

Cultural Performances

Opportunities to participate in or attend French music, dance, and theatrical performances throughout the academic year.

Exchange Programs

Partnerships with French universities and cultural institutions for study abroad opportunities and virtual exchanges.

Competitions

Participation in national and international French language competitions, showcasing our members' talents and skills.

Recent Achievements

National Recognition

Our debate team won first place in the National French Debate Competition, competing against 15 universities across the country.

Three of our members were selected for the prestigious Assistant de Langue program to teach English in French schools.

Cultural Impact

Our annual French Cultural Festival attracted over 500 participants from TUK and surrounding communities.

We established a new partnership with Alliance Française to provide discounted language courses and DELF/DALF exam preparation.
